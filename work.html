LetsMeet

React, Node, oAuth, Serverless, PWA, Jest, Gherkin, Puppeteer

Experience in developing a serverless React app with OAuth integration, featuring Lambda functions for reading and organizing conference meeting times from a calendar. Implemented a searchable data format and incorporated charts for a user-friendly display. Utilized a comprehensive testing approach, including Test-Driven Development (TDD), Behavior-Driven Development (BDD), and End-to-End Acceptance Tests. While the app may not boast flashy visuals, the repository reveals extensive behind-the-scenes work, resulting in a responsive, robust, and easily maintainable solution.
